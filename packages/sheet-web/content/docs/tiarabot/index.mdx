TiaraBot connects an event server’s schedule sheet to Discord. It exposes individual fill schedules, open slots, check-ins, schedule screenshots, room order, saved fill-team information, and service readiness.

It does **not** choose a runner’s team, spend energy or crystals, play Envy/LnF/Melt/HCM, join a private co-op room, assign an open slot, or write your server’s emergency ping.

## Check-in timing and procedure

TiaraBot provides the same check-in procedure whether the check-in is automatic or manually triggered. With auto check-in enabled, TiaraBot schedules the check-in timing. A server that keeps its own timing can have mana/moni run `/checkin manual` instead.

Other procedures can vary. Servers may send schedule-sign-up/open-slot pings earlier or later. Some use standby well before a slot and escalate to emergency. Others go directly to emergency. TiaraBot supplies schedule and slot data for either approach without choosing the server’s escalation policy.

## Team submission availability

<Callout type="warn" title="Unstable, server-gated feature">
  Team submission is enabled or disabled per server by **Theerie**, the bot owner. If your server
  has no monitored team-submission channel or confirmation controls, use its established manual
  process. The rest of TiaraBot remains usable.
</Callout>
