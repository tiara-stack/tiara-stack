title: Read your fill schedule
description: Use /schedule list and /slot to inspect your hours and current openings.
---

## Check your assigned hours

Run `/schedule list` with:

- `day`: Required.
- `user`: Omit it to look up yourself.
- `server_id`: Only use this when you need to target another installed event server.

TiaraBot replies ephemerally with the selected account’s **Fill**, **Overfill**, and **Standby** hour ranges. If the schedule is still hidden, it says the schedule is not visible yet.

## Know the boundary

`/schedule list` is an individual lookup. It does not show the full schedule image, identify the runner, provide a room code, or change an assignment.

If an hour is missing, duplicated, or conflicts with another fill shift, capture the response and contact your server’s mana/moni with the day and hour.

## Inspect openings

`/slot list day:<day>` returns **Open Slots** and **Filled Slots**. A server may also post an **Open slots** button generated by `/slot button`. Pressing it refreshes the current open-slot response.

Viewing an opening does not claim it or update the sheet. Respond through your server’s fill/standby process.
