## Follow TiaraBot’s check-in

This is the canonical check-in prompt. The names, channel, hour, and timestamp change with the
schedule.

<CheckinPromptExample />

The check-in may be scheduled automatically by TiaraBot or manually triggered by mana/moni at your server’s chosen time. In either case, when the check-in message or DM arrives:

1. Verify the displayed hour and running-room context.
2. Press **Check in** once.
3. Wait for the private confirmation shown below. If you already responded, TiaraBot says so.
4. Open the running channel. When the server uses a room-access role, the check-in grants it.
5. Follow the host’s pinned room order and in-game room code.

### Confirmation shown after pressing Check in

This is a private response to your button press, not a new message for the channel.

### Updated check-in list

The original message is updated in the check-in channel so everyone can see who has responded.

<CheckedInPromptExample />

### Running-room announcement

On success, TiaraBot records your response, reapplies the running-room role when one is configured, updates the check-in list, and announces your check-in in the running room.

<CheckinAnnouncementExample />

<Callout title="What the button does not verify">
  Check-in does not validate your in-game team, ISV, Encore setup, song/difficulty, network, or
  presence in the private co-op room. Complete the room’s existing readiness steps after pressing
  it.
</Callout>

`/checkin manual` creates the check-in flow and belongs to mana/moni. It is not a filler fallback. If the button is wrong or fails, contact your server’s mana/moni.
