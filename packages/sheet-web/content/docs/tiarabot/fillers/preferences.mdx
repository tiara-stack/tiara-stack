---
title: Configure TiaraBot check-in DMs
description: Use /preference for notification delivery. It does not set fill availability or team preferences.
---

Use the `/preference checkin-dm` group to inspect, enable, or disable TiaraBot check-in DMs. Use the DM-client setting when your server operates more than one Tiara client and mana/moni tells you which one to select.

## What this setting controls

- whether TiaraBot sends your check-in reminder by DM.
- which configured bot client delivers supported DMs.

## What it does not control

`/preference` does not store your availability, Fill/Overfill/Standby hours, runner, room, fill team, EB, ISV, Encore role, or song/difficulty preferences. Schedule changes still go through your server’s mana/moni.
